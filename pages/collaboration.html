If you are willing to collaborate with me, here is what you may want to know:

What can I provide?

  • Idea. I have a lot of interesting ideas about (M)LLM reasoning, LLM parameter-efficient fine-tuning (PEFT), LLM Agent, etc. If you have your own ideas, we can discuss.
  • Paper writing. I can provide part of paper writing.
  • Guidance. I can guide you in all aspects, such as experiments, writing, rebuttal (revision or response for journal), etc.
  • GPUs. I have several high-performance GPU stations. It would be even better if you have your own GPU.

What are the expectations for you?

  • Self-motivated
  • Have some hands-on experience in the field of LLM, such as LLM training
  • Having published papers is a major plus, even the paper is not in top venues.

Upon confirmation of our collaboration, your responsibilities will include:

  • Implement the methods or algorithms, you will write the codes.
  • Conduct the experiments
  • Write part of papers

Goals

  • Do meaningful and impactful work
  • Publish papers in top conference such as NeurIPS, ICML, ICLR, ACL, CVPR, etc. (You will be the first or co-first author)
  • Produce open-source projects.
  • Thereby benefiting each other.
